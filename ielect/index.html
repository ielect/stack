What is i-Elect?

i-Elect is a blockchain-based survey, election and polling ecosystem allowing anyone anywhere to create, engage and educate online from a digital device, gathering people powered data in a fully secure, easy and reliable way.

i-Elect effectively protects the identities of users anonymous and generating analyzed data for informed decisions through our unique data algorithms. Users earn points based on engagements and interactions thereby increasing community activity. We believe in revolutionizing the educational system and wanted to share our part in building the future of Education with the power of Big Data.

The i-Elect Framework

At the core of ielect is the etheruem solidty framework, a stack of Ethereum smart contracts and front-end libraries distributed on linode which power a district's basic functionalities.

i-Elect is designed to be open and extendable, allowing districts to implement unique features and enhanced functionality via the plug-in of auxiliary modules.

BigData by i-Elect

Data is the foundation of all analytics. i-Elect is driving the incorporation of advanced data technologies to fine-tune the educational process. This platform is creating a competitive edge for students, businesses, virtual organizations and entities. i-Elect will be a major frontier of big data in the educational and learning space both because of its human-facing nature and how crucial it is to society. Analyzing this data trail in real-time will help gain a better understanding of the individual behavior of students, and in creating an optimal learning environment for the students.

Why i-Elect?

Personalized and Customized Learning

Big data technology is supporting the emergence of new educational models, such as digital learning and customized curricula. Most modern consumers are extremely reliant on technology. Today’s smartphones, cars and appliances aid consumers in navigating the world and managing the basic necessities of living. With i-Elect, educators use big data technology to collect information about students and develop customized learning plans.



Informed Data

With data at the heart of education, i-Elect is envisioning a data driven classroom with detailed information that will drive decisions of students and learning communities in professional learning. The data driven classroom will steer individuals and whole-group teaching strategies based on common educational data. With data there's no guesswork required.

Increase in Participation

Generally, there has been a decline of interest in undertaking formal education, since surveys have shown that degrees simply aren't the guarantees to a better future that they once were. With big data and the ability to customize the content and timelines of courses to suit one's needs, more students are entering MOOCs and distance-learning courses. i-Elect will be a major frontier of big data in the educational sector both because of its human-facing nature and how crucial it is to society.

Anonymity

It is a common misperception that blockchain networks like bitcoin are anonymous, when in fact they are only confidential. That is, when a user makes public transactions, their unique code called a public key, is recorded on the blockchain, rather than their personal information. .

The i-Elect Blockchain Network Token

To provide all internet citizens with an opportunity to freely join and contribute to any Public Survey or Poll, to align incentives across all participants, and to implement coordinated decision-making mechanisms to the i-Elect Network, we introduce the i-Elect Network Token.

The i-Elect Network Token is a multi-utility token which is required for application to the i-Elect Network, utilized in an incentivized voting game to dictate access to a suite of ancillary services exclusive to network members, used to signal support or disapproval for proposals made by network participants, and which can be staked to deposit pools to mint tokens providing participants voting/survey rights.

Voting rights can be used to partake in the processes that determine everything from a Survey or poll design and branding, to its functionality, to its accepted code of conduct, to the integrations it utilizes, and beyond.

What Should We Build Next?

We encourage and incentivize the submission of marketplace ideas via an open community proposal process. The i-Elect Network Token allows holders to signal for what markets they would like to see the i-Elect team build and deploy to the network next.

The i-Elect Registry

The i-Elect Registry is a decentrally maintained district whitelist, storing the credentials of districts which have been granted access to the i-Elect Network. Based on an incentivized voting game called a Token-Curated Registry, It signifies that Participants has been accredited by i-Elect as non-malicious and value-additive to the i-Elect Network.
